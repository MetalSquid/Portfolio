A Little About Me

HEADSHOT

I have always had a love for technology and the internet, and my curiostity led me to learning how websites work. I fell in love with the process and with the satisfaction of creating something like a dynamic webpage from essentially nothing and the ability to do so with relatively few tools. A lot of the HTML for these portfolio projects I wrote in Notepad while staying late after work.

On this website you will see work examples, links to my Github and LinkedIn accounts and a little bit about me. Before my interest in development I used to work as a chef, and then a registered dietitian, where I would travel and work in various acute care hospitals during the covid 19 pandemic. I found that while working in healthcare is rewarding, my true passion lies in tech and creating.
